Our Events

A brief Summary of what our events are about

In the last one year, we had events ranging from various internal technical workshops to many external workshops, giving hands on experience to the maximum number of people that we could. The previous year began with a website development workshop that attracted a huge crowd, which was followed by the ManComm recruitments. A new Management Committee was assigned to take the ISTE club forward, which was again followed by Working Committee recruitments and we welcomed a new working committee for the club. After this we had Acumen, which is the category with highest participation in TechTatva. It had several categories within it including MIQ, AIB, hopeless opus, ATR etc. Next we had the Swacch Bharat mission event. Next we had the most successful workshop that got in a huge number of students from different years and many branches. We also organised the winter school which was an online school where we taught different skills and also provided e-certificates to those who performed well. We then organised a tech week which had different categories including Wattup, codestorm, clueless,unlockathon, exquisite.

We then organised an internal workshop for graphics and advanced python. We then had Alexa phase 2 workshop that again attracted a huge number. We also organised an internal workshop for MATLAB. This was followed by summer school which had many domains for people of different interests. It had web dev, python, graphics, app dev , data structures. Apart from this we also conducted many doubt clearing sessions and small internal workshops for club. One of the most imperative things that we had done this year was to start out own blog- THE TECHNICAL HEAD.
